About the Tournament

The Arcade Realm Clash of Champions brings together the top gamers from around the world to compete in an epic Valorant showdown across India. Test your skills, strategize with your team, and battle your way to the top for a chance to win amazing prizes and global recognition.

Rules & Guidelines

  • All participants must be 16 years or older.
  • Fair play is mandatory; any form of cheating will result in disqualification.
  • Teams should consist of 5 members.
  • Respect and sportsmanship are expected at all times.
  • There will be in-game qualifiers first.
  • Top 64 teams will play LAN event which is going to held in CHANDIGARH.
